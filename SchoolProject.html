Tipping Point

The Tipping Point is a 3D hack-n-slash action-adventure game that is set in a fantasy world that’s named Eregor, where its inhabitants are fighting an endless war amongst themselves out of ignorance, hatred, and thirst for power.

Project summary

The Tipping Point aims to immerse players in a story that deals with modern issues of corruption and moral decay. This work depicts a world in which the protagonist faces the temptations of power and the consequences of falling for it. The game invites players to confront the complexities of corruption and its consequences through a combination of war scenarios and dark fantasy.

Prototype Details

  • Playable gameplay albeit low quality characters
  • Storyline/Cutscenes completed
